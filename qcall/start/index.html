QCALL Checkout Terminal

QCALL Checkout Terminal

QCALL Checkout Terminal is the perfect solution for processing written or telephone order payment data, i.e. credit card or bank details needed for SEPA Direct Debit transactions. Payment details of your consumers are recorded in the web-based, password protected QCALL Checkout Terminal interface and are processed automatically and immediately during the telephone order process. All successful transactions are available in the QPC Payment Center. Currently we support Credit Card, SEPA Direct Debit and recurring payment.

Benefits and features

  • Immediate confirmation that the payment has been processed while the consumer is still on the phone, i.e. any incorrect information or typing mistakes can be rectified at once.

  • Processing of all incoming payment data with immediate success control.

  • Payments for orders received by fax or mail can also be processed immediately to complete the orders.

  • Perfectly suited to carry out recurring payments (e.g. subscriptions).

  • QCALL Checkout Terminal runs in any web browser and no additional software installation is required on your side.
