Non-transaction-based operations

Non-transaction-based operations

Non-transaction-based operations for Wirecard Checkout Page and Wirecard Checkout Seamless do not depend on existing orders. I.e. such operations are carried out independently of any existing orders.

Available non-transaction-based operations

The following non-transaction-based operations are available:

Operation Description

generateOrderNumber

For creating a new order number in the payment system.

getFinancialInstitutions

For querying possible and supported financial institutions of a certain payment method.

Please be aware that the availability of a non-transaction-based operation depends on

  • The payment method

  • The financial institution

If you wish to use such non-transaction-based operations you need to purchase these features which are then enabled for you from our side. If you have any questions please contact our sales teams.

Non-transaction-based operations and payment methods

You can find a detailed table displaying all supported payment methods and their corresponding non-transaction-based operations at non-transaction-based operations by payment method.
